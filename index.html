Microcontroller Expertise

NRF5340

Advanced dual-core SoC with dedicated application and network processing cores based on ARM Cortex-M33 architecture.

Core: Dual Cortex-M33

Flash: 1MB

RAM: 512KB

Connectivity: BLE 5.2, Thread, Zigbee

Security: ARM TrustZone, Root of Trust

NRF52840

Powerful single-core SoC with extensive wireless capabilities and advanced power management for IoT applications.

Core: Cortex-M4F

Flash: 1MB

RAM: 256KB

Connectivity: BLE 5.0, Thread, Zigbee

Security: Crypto Accelerator, AES

STM32H7 Series

High-performance MCU series with state-of-the-art processing power and extensive peripheral options for demanding applications.

Core: Cortex-M7 (+M4 in dual-core)

Speed: Up to 480 MHz

Flash: Up to 2MB

RAM: Up to 1MB

Peripherals: Ethernet, USB OTG, CAN FD

STM32F7 Series

High-performance MCUs designed for applications requiring fast processing and rich connectivity with excellent power efficiency.

Core: Cortex-M7

Speed: Up to 216 MHz

Flash: Up to 2MB

RAM: Up to 512KB

Peripherals: LCD-TFT, Camera, Crypto

ESP32

Feature-rich SoC with integrated Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity designed for a wide range of IoT applications.

Core: Dual-core Xtensa LX6

Speed: Up to 240 MHz

Flash: External, up to 16MB

RAM: 520KB SRAM

Connectivity: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 4.2

CC3200

Wi-Fi enabled microcontroller from Texas Instruments designed for Internet of Things applications with built-in security features.

Core: Cortex-M4

Speed: 80 MHz

Flash: 1MB

RAM: 256KB

Connectivity: Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n
